Beside

Experience

Users can view ride recordings of specific route uploaded by other users. Bike newbies can sort of know how intense riding specific route is by watching the recordings and reading other users' body statistic while they rode that route. After "experiencing" the route by watching the recordings on phone/web/ipad or their VR headset, user then can decide whether they are strong enough to challenge that route. You can also connect your Beside App to a bike training machine via Bluetooth and sync specific route elevation in the machine. Put on your VR headset and train like you are actually at the mountains overseas!

Share

Share your ride experiences with your friends or other bikers! Challenge your friend, Have a race, Be the top of the leaderboard.

Explore

Download the Beside app so you can directly upload or record your ride recordings in the app. You can choose a route from the map in the app and start riding.

Colour Palette

The aquamarine color is chosen to create a chill and relaxing vibe. The white and the dark grey is for contrasting the aquamarine without making the app too busy.

 

Chosen Font

Tamil MN is chosen to be the main font of the Beside appp. It presents a lifestyle vibe.
